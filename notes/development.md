# Development Notes

## Origin

QCE emerged from a series of conversations and independent research during 2025-2026. The core insight: if MWI is true, then a person is literally a distributed entity, and ethics must account for what happens across the full distribution — not just within a single branch.

## Key Connections Still To Explore

- **Constructor theory (Deutsch/Marletto):** Can constructor-theoretic possibility formalize Sigma?
- **Coleridge's imagination:** The interface between embodied self and cognitive prosthesis (including LLMs) as secondary imagination — the same faculty that mediates internal cognition.
- **Amendment Zero:** The legal argument that LLM conversations constitute prosthetic cognition and deserve absolute protection as thought, not speech or data.
- **Decision theory:** Relationship between QCE softmax and existing frameworks (causal decision theory, evidential decision theory, functional decision theory).
- **Biological grounding of Triple P:** Can P, Sigma, Lambda be operationalized through measurable quantities?
